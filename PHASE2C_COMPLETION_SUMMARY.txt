Purpose: Calculate 4-gate validation system for Phase Q1 go-live.

The 4 Gates:
  Gate 1: Win Rate >= 40%
          Measures: winners / total_trades
          Why: Baseline profitability requirement

  Gate 2: Rung Hits >= 60%
          Measures: trades_hitting_rung_2+ / total_trades
          Why: Risk control — most trades must reach breakeven

  Gate 3: Profit Factor >= 1.5x
          Measures: gross_wins / gross_loss
          Why: Edge validation — wins must be 1.5x larger than losses

  Gate 4: Max Losing Streak <= 3
          Measures: longest_consecutive_losses
          Why: Psychology — prevent emotional spiral trades

  • calculate_gates(trades)        → Full 4-gate calculation
