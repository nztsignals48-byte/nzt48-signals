RISK ASSESSMENT & MITIGATIONS
================================================================================

RISK 1: Signal Explosion (Too Many Signals)
  Severity: MEDIUM
  Trigger: >50 signals/day output
  Mitigation: Signal fusion (Bayesian aggregation Book 209) + soft Kelly caps
  Monitor: Alert on signal_count > 40/day

RISK 2: Overfitting (In-Sample Discovery)
  Severity: MEDIUM
  Trigger: Phase 2+ signals fail live
  Mitigation: Walk-forward validation (Book 192), Sharpe deflation, out-of-sample test
  Monitor: IC stability check nightly; auto-suspend if IC < 0.02

RISK 3: Data Quality (Stale NAV, Bad Feeds)
  Severity: LOW (existing resilience)
  Trigger: Latency arb getting filled at bad prices
  Mitigation: 5-min hold max, 5% slippage buffer, pre-position on large discounts
  Monitor: Track fill quality vs expected execution cost

RISK 4: Correlation Breakdowns (Pairs Trading)
  Severity: MEDIUM
  Trigger: Cointegrated pairs suddenly diverge
  Mitigation: Daily correlation refresh, auto-exit on R² drop
  Monitor: Track correlation dislocation events

RISK 5: Macro Nowcast Timing (Pre-announcement Volatility)
  Severity: MEDIUM
  Trigger: False signals in 30-min window before data release
  Mitigation: Wait 2-3 bars post-announcement before entry
  Monitor: Track announcement-correlated losses

OVERALL RISK: LOW-MEDIUM (Existing infrastructure handles most edge cases)
